Aaliyah Miller

Backstory

Aaliyah Miller grew up in Brookyln, New York. She is not close to her birth family, but has a great relationship with her adopted parents. She is 27 years old. She seen a lot of crime in the area she grew up in which established her career choices. At the age of 18, she went to New York Law School. Now, she works her first year in the BAU as a Supervisory Special Agent. She tends to sympathize with teens and children who commit crimes because of her upbringing.

Accomplishments

Aaliyah Miller is a Supervisory Special Agent of the FBI's Behavioral Analysis Unit. She graduated top of her class in High School.

Skills

The skills she has are profiling, speaking 4 different languages, and public speaking.

Hobbies/Interests

She likes studying Human Behavior, spending time with children, and learning different languages.
